Month of Vyrtex at Penn!
Vyrtex is a social website to share and organize the best of what you're reading. We're launching at Penn and celebrating by throwing a bunch of awesome events during the month of September.

Our discussion series and speaker events are open for everyone to attend. To claim the perks and drink specials at Thirsty Thursdays, you'll need to show your Vyrtex profile so make sure to signup for Vyrtex!
